Journey of Regret

Scene: Rohan and Neel meeting after a long time

Rohan: You are going for office? Where??

Neel: In an IT company as a software engineer.

Rohan: Oh wow, software nice, but Neel, I remember you always wanted to be a great actor since childhood, right? And you were playing characters at theaters too. What about that?

Neel: Oh acting....heh, I realized that was just not my cup of tea. It was just for entertaining.

Neel smiles in pain, remembering the flashback:

Flashback:

Neel's parents, angry and impatient, stood before him, their hands on their hips. Neel, a young man with dreams of acting, cowered beneath their disapproving gaze.

Parents: "This acting nonsense! You murmur about it constantly, as if it’s some sort of gift from the gods! We are a middle-class family, do you understand? A stable job is what holds us together, not your fantasy of treading the boards."

With the flashback over, Neel’s smile fades, his expression darkened by shadows of regret and disappointment.
